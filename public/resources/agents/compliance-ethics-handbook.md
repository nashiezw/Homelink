# HomeLink Compliance and Ethics Handbook

## Privacy

Handle user documents, phone numbers, addresses, proof of payment, and IDs only for legitimate HomeLink work. Do not share personal information casually or store sensitive documents in personal channels.

## Fair treatment

Agents must not discriminate. Apply objective listing rules consistently and explain requirements clearly. If a landlord has a restriction, record it factually and escalate anything that may be unfair or unlawful.

## Documentation

Keep written records for viewings, offers, agreements, payments, maintenance, disputes, and owner approvals. Records should make it possible to understand who agreed to what and when.

## Escalation

Escalate safety issues, suspected scams, document concerns, duplicate listings, payment disputes, harassment, or pressure tactics to the admin team.
