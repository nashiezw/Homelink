# HomeLink Property Management Operations Guide

## Intake

Capture the owner, property address, service type, urgency, photos, access notes, budget expectations, and preferred communication channel.

## Quotation and approval

Use clear line items. Do not begin paid work without owner approval unless an emergency process applies. Attach quotes and approval notes to the request.

## Delivery

Track contractor details, dates, completion notes, invoices, receipts, and owner feedback. Close the request only when completion evidence is attached.

## Communication

Keep owners updated at each milestone: request received, quote prepared, work approved, work started, completed, invoiced, and closed.
