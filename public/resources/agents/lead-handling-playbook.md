# HomeLink Lead Handling Playbook

## Response standard

Respond to fresh leads quickly and record every meaningful action in HomeLink. Confirm the user's budget, preferred location, move date, contact details, and viewing availability.

## Safe viewing process

1. Confirm the property is still available.
2. Confirm the person showing the property has authority.
3. Share clear time, location, and access instructions.
4. Encourage users to verify details before paying.
5. Record the viewing result and next step.

## Fraud warnings

- Urgent request for money to an unverified account.
- Refusal to allow viewing or verification.
- Photos that do not match the location or property description.
- Duplicate listing with different contact details.
- Pressure to move records away from HomeLink.

## Status discipline

Use accurate statuses. Open leads need action. Closed-won leads need deal details. Closed-lost leads should include a reason. This protects commission records and improves reporting.
