# HomeLink Listing Quality Checklist

## Before publishing

- Confirm the landlord, owner, agency, or property manager has authority to list.
- Confirm price, deposit, fees, availability date, and viewing access.
- Confirm city, suburb, street guidance, and nearby landmarks.
- Record water, power, internet, parking, security, and transport details.
- Ask for restrictions such as pets, smoking, occupancy, or lease duration.

## Media

- Use current photos from the actual property.
- Capture exterior, entrance, kitchen, bathroom, bedrooms, lounge, and parking.
- Use landscape photos where possible and avoid heavy filters.
- Do not hide damage, unfinished work, shared spaces, or access restrictions.

## Listing copy

- Title must describe the real property and location.
- Description must be factual and specific.
- Amenities must match what was verified.
- House rules and fees must be clear.
- If something is uncertain, mark it for review instead of guessing.

## Final review

- No duplicate listing already exists.
- Photos match the title and suburb.
- Price and availability are current.
- Contact and viewing instructions are correct.
