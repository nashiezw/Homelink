# HomeLink Commission and Growth Guide

## Commission basics

Commission records depend on lead source, deal type, rule snapshots, and recorded outcomes. Close leads accurately so earnings can be calculated correctly.

## Lead source

- HomeLink-sourced leads come through platform marketing, search, enquiries, or assigned workflows.
- Agent-sourced leads come from the agent's own network and are recorded in HomeLink.
- Referral and recurring commissions follow active admin rules.

## Reviews

Strong reviews come from fast communication, accurate listings, safe viewings, complete records, and respectful follow-up.

## Growth

Agents can improve performance through completed training, verified listings, closed deals, positive reviews, clean documentation, and low dispute rates.
