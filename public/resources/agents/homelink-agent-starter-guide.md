# HomeLink Agent Starter Guide

## Purpose

HomeLink agents help renters, buyers, landlords, property owners, and property managers complete safer property journeys. Every agent must protect trust, keep records inside HomeLink, and communicate clearly.

## Daily workflow

1. Check new leads and enquiries.
2. Confirm property availability before arranging viewings.
3. Keep all lead notes, viewing outcomes, and next steps updated.
4. Escalate suspicious listings, payment requests, or user behaviour.
5. Close deals only after the key facts, parties, and records are confirmed.

## Conduct standards

- Be punctual for calls, viewings, inspections, and owner meetings.
- Use respectful, non-discriminatory language.
- Do not ask users to move important records away from HomeLink.
- Do not share IDs, phone numbers, addresses, or documents outside a legitimate workflow.
- Never pressure a user to pay before they have completed reasonable due diligence.

## Minimum records

- Lead source and client details.
- Property viewed or requested.
- Viewing date, outcome, and next step.
- Any documents, agreements, receipts, or payment proof.
- Safety concerns or disputes.
